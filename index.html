About Us

REDMOND'S
LEGENDARY
BAR & GRILL

Nestled on Leary Way in the heart of Redmond, Palmer's East isn't just a bar — it's a community institution. For over two decades, we've been the Eastside's go-to spot for cold drinks, hot food, and the longest-running karaoke show on the east side of Lake Washington.

Whether you're a local regular or a first-time visitor, you'll feel right at home. No pretense, no fluff — just great bartenders with personality, ice-cold beers, strong drinks, and a karaoke stage that's seen more talent than most concert halls.
